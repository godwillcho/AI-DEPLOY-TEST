Partnership Levels

Premium

Full access to all Thrive@Work programs including emergency financial assistance, wellness programs, and priority scheduling for financial coaching. Includes quarterly wellness reports for the employer (aggregated, non-identifying data only).

Standard

Access to EAP counseling, financial wellness coaching, and community resource referrals. Does not include emergency financial assistance or specialized wellness programs.

Basic

Access to EAP counseling and 211 community resource referrals. Financial coaching available on a limited basis.

Becoming a Partner

Employers interested in offering Thrive@Work to their employees can contact Trident United Way's corporate partnerships team. Partnerships are customizable based on employer size, budget, and employee needs.
