211 HELPLINE
===================================================================
Phone: Dial 2-1-1 (or 866-892-2114 from cell phones)
Hours: 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
Services: Information and referral for any need — housing, food, health,
  utilities, crisis support, and more
Text: Text your ZIP code to 898-211
Website: sc211.org

AFTER HOURS
===================================================================
If you need help outside of business hours:
- Use the Stability360 AI chat (available 24/7)
- Call 211 (available 24/7)
- For emergencies, call 911
- For mental health crisis, call or text 988
- For domestic violence, call the National Hotline: 1-800-799-7233
- For veteran crisis, call 988 and press 1
