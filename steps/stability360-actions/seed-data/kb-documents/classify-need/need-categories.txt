Stability360 Need Classification Guide

When a caller states a need, classify it into ONE category below.
Match the caller's words against the keywords listed. Choose the category
with the longest keyword match. NEVER show the caller a list of categories.

ROUTE R CATEGORIES (lighter intake):

Food Assistance (category: food)
  Keywords: food, hungry, groceries, food pantry, meals, snap, food stamps, wic

Health and Medical (category: health)
  Keywords: health, medical, doctor, dental, mental health, counseling, therapy, prescription, medicine, insurance

Transportation (category: transport)
  Keywords: transport, transportation, ride, bus, car

Childcare (category: childcare)
  Keywords: childcare, child care, daycare, babysitter, after school

Employment (category: employment)
  Keywords: employment, job, work, resume, career, training

Legal (category: legal)
  Keywords: legal, lawyer, attorney, court, custody, divorce

Financial (category: financial)
  Keywords: financial, debt, credit, budget, taxes, tax

Home Repairs (category: repairs)
  Keywords: repairs, home repair, fix

Disaster Relief (category: disaster)
  Keywords: disaster, flood, hurricane, storm, fire

Hygiene and Supplies (category: hygiene)
  Keywords: hygiene, diapers, toiletries, cleaning

ROUTE D CATEGORIES (deeper intake — housing/income questions required):

Utilities (category: utilities)
  Keywords: utilities, electric, electricity, water, gas, power, electric bill, water bill, gas bill, light bill, shutoff, shut off, disconnection
  NOTE: If classified as utilities, ask the caller which type (Electric/Power, Water, or Gas)

Rental Assistance (category: rental)
  Keywords: rental, rent, rent assistance, behind on rent

Shelter (category: shelter)
  Keywords: shelter, homeless, housing, place to stay, no home

Eviction Prevention (category: eviction)
  Keywords: eviction, evicted, eviction notice, kicked out

EMERGENCY KEYWORDS:
If ANY of these appear in the caller's words, mark as emergency:
shutoff, shut off, disconnection, no power, no electricity, homeless, no home,
on the street, no food, starving, no water, kicked out, sleeping outside,
no place to stay, emergency, crisis, urgent

Emergency procedure: Fast-track — collect name, ZIP, contact only, then search immediately.

If no keywords match, ask the caller to describe their need in different words.
