EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (EAP) — Expanded Services
Stability360 Thrive@Work by Trident United Way

OVERVIEW
The Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is available to employees of
Thrive@Work partner employers.  EAP provides confidential, short-term
support for personal and work-related challenges.  Services are provided
at no cost to the employee.

SERVICES AVAILABLE

1. COUNSELING SESSIONS
   - Up to 6 confidential counseling sessions per issue per year
   - Available in-person, by phone, or via video
   - Covers: stress, anxiety, depression, grief, relationship issues,
     workplace conflict, life transitions
   - Sessions provided by licensed clinical social workers and counselors
   - Immediate crisis support available 24/7

2. FINANCIAL COACHING
   - One-on-one sessions with a certified financial coach
   - Budgeting and expense management
   - Debt reduction strategies
   - Savings plans and emergency fund building
   - Credit repair guidance
   - Understanding benefits and retirement planning
   - Up to 4 sessions per year

3. LEGAL CONSULTATION
   - Initial 30-minute consultation with a licensed attorney
   - Covers: family law, landlord-tenant disputes, consumer rights,
     immigration questions, estate planning basics
   - Does NOT cover: ongoing representation, criminal defense, active
     lawsuits against the employer
   - Referral to reduced-fee legal services if ongoing help needed

4. WORK-LIFE BALANCE RESOURCES
   - Child care referrals and resources
   - Elder care information and planning
   - College planning resources
   - Relocation assistance information
   - Community resource navigation

HOW TO ACCESS EAP
- Employee must be verified through the Thrive@Work employee lookup
- After verification, employee can request EAP services directly
- All services are confidential — employer is not notified of usage
- Employee only needs to provide their employee ID to get started

ELIGIBILITY
- Must be an active employee of a Thrive@Work partner employer
- Partnership status must be ACTIVE
- EAP must be included in the employer's selected program package
- Household members of eligible employees may also access services
  (varies by employer agreement)

CONFIDENTIALITY
- All EAP interactions are strictly confidential
- Employer receives only aggregate usage statistics (no individual data)
- Exceptions to confidentiality: imminent danger to self or others,
  suspected child or elder abuse (mandatory reporting), court order
- The AI system does not store session content from EAP interactions

CONTACT
- Through the Stability360 chat system (verify employee ID first)
- EAP Direct Line: (843) 740-7050
- Available Monday-Friday 8 AM – 6 PM, crisis support 24/7
