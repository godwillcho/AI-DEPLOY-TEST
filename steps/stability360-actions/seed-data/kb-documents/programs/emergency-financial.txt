EMERGENCY FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE — Stability360 by Trident United Way

OVERVIEW
Stability360 provides emergency financial assistance for tri-county residents
facing urgent financial hardship.  This is a direct support service — cases
are managed by the Stability360 team.

WHAT IS COVERED
- Emergency rent assistance (to prevent eviction)
- Emergency utility payments (to prevent shutoff)
- Emergency prescription medication costs
- Emergency transportation for medical appointments
- Emergency food assistance beyond food bank referrals

ELIGIBILITY
- Must reside in Berkeley, Charleston, or Dorchester County
- Must demonstrate urgent financial need (past-due notice, eviction notice, etc.)
- Income generally at or below 200% of the Federal Poverty Level
- One-time assistance per household per 12-month period (exceptions for
  documented emergencies)
- Must not have received the same type of assistance from another agency in
  the past 30 days

HOW TO APPLY
1. Contact Stability360 via chat, phone, or walk-in
2. Complete the intake assessment (the AI system will collect all needed info)
3. Provide required documentation
4. A case manager reviews the application within 24-48 hours
5. If approved, payment is made directly to the vendor (landlord, utility
   company, pharmacy, etc.)

WHAT TO BRING / WHAT IS NEEDED
- Photo ID
- Proof of income (pay stubs, SSI letter, unemployment letter)
- Proof of residency (lease, utility bill in your name)
- Documentation of the emergency (eviction notice, shutoff notice,
  prescription with cost, etc.)
- Vendor contact information (landlord name and phone, utility account number)

PROCESSING TIME
- Standard: 24-48 hours from complete application to decision
- PRIORITY (72-hour shutoff or eviction): Same-day review when possible
- Payment to vendor: 3-5 business days after approval

LIMITS
- Rent assistance: Up to one month's rent (maximum $1,500)
- Utility assistance: Up to $500 per occurrence
- Prescription assistance: Up to $200 per occurrence
- Transportation: Up to $100 per occurrence
