Stability360 Thrive@Work Program Overview

Thrive@Work is an employer-partnered wellness and stability program operated by Trident United Way. It provides employees of participating employers with confidential access to a range of support services designed to improve personal stability, financial wellness, and overall well-being.

Program Categories:
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Confidential counseling and support services for employees and their household members. Covers mental health, substance use, relationship issues, and crisis support. Typically includes up to 6 sessions per issue per year at no cost to the employee.
- Financial Wellness: Financial coaching, budgeting workshops, emergency financial assistance, and connections to community resources for rent, utilities, and food assistance.
- Wellness Programs: Health and wellness initiatives including stress management, mindfulness, work-life balance coaching, and health screenings.
- Community Resources: Access to Trident United Way's 211 network of community service providers for housing, food, childcare, transportation, healthcare, and other essential needs.

How It Works:
1. An employee contacts the Thrive@Work helpline or chat service.
2. The system verifies their employee ID to confirm enrollment.
3. Based on the employee's needs, they are connected to the appropriate program or resource.
4. All interactions are confidential and do not affect employment status.

Employer Partnership:
Employers partner with Trident United Way to offer Thrive@Work as a benefit. The employer covers the cost of the program, and employees access services at no charge. Participating employers include organizations across the Lowcountry region of South Carolina.

Contact:
Employees can access Thrive@Work by calling the dedicated helpline or using the online chat portal. An employee ID is required for verification.
